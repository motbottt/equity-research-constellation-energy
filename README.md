## Overview

This repository contains a team equity research presentation analyzing Constellation Energy (NASDAQ: CEG), completed as part of a University of Wisconsin–Madison investment research project.

Our team evaluated the company's business model, competitive positioning, industry outlook, financial performance, valuation, and long-term growth opportunities to determine whether the stock represented an attractive investment.

**Recommendation:** BUY

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze Constellation Energy's business and operations
- Evaluate the U.S. energy and nuclear power industry
- Assess growth opportunities driven by clean energy and AI-related electricity demand
- Perform comparable company valuation
- Develop a long-term investment recommendation
